# Deploy to Netlify (Free)

## Step 1: Create Netlify Account
1. Go to https://netlify.com
2. Click "Sign up" → "Sign up with GitHub" (or email)
3. Verify your email

## Step 2: Drag & Drop Deployment
1. Go to https://app.netlify.com/drop
2. Drag your entire `d:\travi` folder into the drop zone
3. Wait for deployment (2-3 minutes)
4. Get your free URL: `https://your-site-name.netlify.app`

## Step 3: Custom Domain (Optional)
1. In Netlify dashboard → Site settings → Domain management
2. Add custom domain like `The Pr♦de Holidays.com`
3. Follow DNS instructions

## Benefits:
- ✅ Free hosting
- ✅ HTTPS security
- ✅ Global CDN
- ✅ Custom domain support
- ✅ Automatic deployments
- ✅ No server maintenance
